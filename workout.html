Cardio

Cardiovascular exercise, commonly known as cardio, is a vital component of any fitness regimen. By elevating your heart rate and increasing blood circulation, cardio activities such as running, cycling, and swimming promote heart health, improve endurance, and burn calories. Engaging in regular cardio workouts not only strengthens the heart and lungs but also enhances overall physical fitness and mental well-being.
